About Gorilla Jiu Jitsu

Born in the heart of Raasepori, Gorilla Jiu Jitsu is more than a gym — it's a brotherhood forged on the mats. We believe Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is the ultimate vehicle for building discipline, resilience, and an unbreakable mindset.

Our academy welcomes everyone — from complete beginners to seasoned competitors. Whether you're chasing podium finishes or simply seeking a new way to challenge body and mind, the Gorilla family has a place for you.

Step into our world-class training facility, feel the energy of a team that pushes each other to greatness, and discover what you're truly capable of.

What We Teach

🥋

Gi Jiu Jitsu

Traditional BJJ with the kimono. Master grips, sweeps, and submissions using the classic art form that started it all.

💪

No-Gi Grappling

Fast-paced submission wrestling without the gi. Develop explosive takedowns, scrambles, and modern leg-lock systems.

🏆

Competition Training

Sharpen your game for the podium. Intense drilling, positional sparring, and strategy sessions guided by experienced competitors.

🧒

Kids Program

Building confidence, discipline, and coordination in young grapplers aged 5–15. A safe, fun, and empowering environment.
